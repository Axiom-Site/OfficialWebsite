About Eco Lib Foundation

A need to begin such a center was felt when the Founding Secretary visited the Jharsa area saw the appalling conditions in which numerous poor children worked as rag- pickers and were without proper food, clothing, sanitation or education.

initiated its work from Lucknow, in 1996 works for issues of underprivileged, vulnerable and marginalized segments in both rural and urban settings with emphasis on non-formal education, environment conservation, women empowerment and enterprise promotion. Concurrently Eco Lib is running an informal literacy center- "Radiant Kids", in the slums of Jharsa Village, in Sec. 39, in area opposite Medanta Medicity Hospital, where about 120- 125 children in the age group 4 -18 years, both boys as well as girls are enrolled. We impart basic literacy to totally out of school kids of rag- pickers, migrant labourers, maids, vegetable vendors, security guards, etc supplementary education in English, Maths and Computers to working children and to those under-privileged and marginalized (Harijan) children who attend Hindi medium Schools; as well as motivational and value inputs along with a platform for the creative development of all kids through music- both vocal and instrumental, dance, art and craft, yoga, karate,games, library, etc., with the idea of linking them to vocational options. We operate between 3.00- 6.00 pm, Mon. to Sat. Besides this, we also conduct camps regarding health and hygiene, gender sensitization, awareness regarding equality for and prevention of violence against women and girls, self- defence techniques, linking young adults and women to vocational opportunities, and on environmental issues. It is my constant endeavour to provide entertainment, educational and awareness- building opportunities to these children by way of taking them to Raahgiri, seminars/ workshops, planetariums, picnics and to competitions across the city and in Delhi.
